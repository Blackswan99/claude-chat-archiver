Claude Chat Archiver

Welcome to Claude Chat Archiver

Before you continue, please review what this extension does:

📤 What data is transmitted?

This extension reads your chat history from claude.ai and uploads the content (messages, attachments, Claude artifacts) to a GitHub repository that you configure and control.

🔒 Where does your data go?

Data is transmitted directly between:

  • Your browser ↔ claude.ai (via your existing session cookie)
  • Your browser ↔ api.github.com (via your Personal Access Token)

No data is sent to the extension author, third parties, analytics services, or any other destination.

👤 You stay in control

Nothing is uploaded automatically on first install. You explicitly select which chats to archive and click "Archive" to trigger the transfer. Auto-sync is off by default and must be enabled manually.

🔑 Your credentials

Your GitHub token is stored locally in your browser's extension storage. It never leaves your device except in the form of authenticated API requests to GitHub.
